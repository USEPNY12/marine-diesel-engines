# SEO Content Strategy & Plan: US Engines Production Marine Division

This document outlines the comprehensive content strategy for the US Engines Production Marine Division website. The primary objective is to secure the top ranking on Google for high-intent marine diesel engine search terms, thereby driving qualified leads specifically for remanufactured engines.

## 1. SEO Keyword Strategy

Based on an analysis of search volume and buyer intent, our strategy focuses on two main categories of keywords. These keywords are designed to capture both users ready to make a purchase and those in the research phase of their buying journey.

### High-Intent Transactional Keywords

These keywords target users who are actively looking to purchase or price a specific engine. They are critical for the product pages. The primary structure involves combining terms indicating intent with specific brand and model names. For example, targeting phrases like "remanufactured Cummins marine diesel engine" or "rebuilt Caterpillar C18 marine engine for sale" captures direct buying intent. Furthermore, phrases focusing on pricing, such as "Cummins QSM11 marine engine replacement cost," and direct purchase queries like "buy Volvo Penta D6 marine diesel" are essential for capturing bottom-of-the-funnel traffic.

### Informational Keywords

These keywords target users in the research phase and are the primary focus for the blog and resource sections. These queries often take the form of questions or comparisons. Key phrases include broad inquiries like "best marine diesel engine for commercial fishing boat" or direct comparisons such as "Cummins vs Caterpillar marine diesel engine comparison." Additionally, we will target maintenance and lifecycle queries, including "how much does a marine diesel engine cost to replace," "marine diesel engine rebuild vs replace," and "marine diesel engine maintenance schedule."

## 4. Social Media Integration (Instagram & TikTok)

To maximize our reach and engage with a broader audience, the concepts developed for the blog and product highlights will be repurposed into short-form video content. This strategy is specifically tailored for platforms like Instagram Reels and TikTok.

Video concepts will include "Dyno Test Day" segments, which feature short clips showing remanufactured engines running on the dynamometer before shipping to demonstrate quality assurance. We will also produce "Before & After" visual transformations, showcasing the journey of old engine cores being rebuilt into pristine remanufactured units. Finally, "Spec Check" videos will provide rapid, 30-second overviews of specific engine models, highlighting key selling points.
